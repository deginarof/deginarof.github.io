Alignment

Magazine Luiza Store

Magazine Luiza

Magazine Luiza is an online store that sells pretty much everything, but it is very popular for selling eletronics. Its page is very colorful and the alignment is mostly vertical. In some parts of the page we can notice a top-bottom vertical alignment and in other parts just the centered one.

Repetition

Constance Store

Constance

Constance is an online store that sells very good shoes and purses. Its collection focus a lot on rough material like ropes, we can see a repetition of colors, pictures and textures that remind us of that.

Contrast

Americanas Store

Lojas Americanas

Lojas Americanas as Magazine Luiza is a department store online. We can see that the constrast is made through the entire page with strong and powerful colors as black, red, green and white. The green color highlight the sales of the day while the red one calls our attentioin to the page menu.
